Apache Sunset (Agastache rupestris)

Next Sale: Spring Garden Fair & Plant Sale May 20th, 2018 (Ithaca High School)

Features Details
Type Perennial
Zone 5-8
When it Blooms June-July
Where to Grow Full sun
Soil Type Well draining, drought resistant
When to divide Resents division
Habit Height: 24” (36” with flower stalk), Spread: 12”-24”, Clumping, occasionally reseeds
Scent Hint of root beer
Attracts Butterflies and Hummingbirds
Resists Deer and rabbits
Maturity Rate Flowers first year, small first year. Reaches full size second year
Long Lived or Short Lived Long lived
Something Special or Unique Flowers edible, very sweet, leaves edible with hint of licorice, root beer
Special care Does not like wet winter soil, mulch to help winter over, do not trim back until late spring-old wood helps protect crown for the winter
