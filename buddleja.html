Butterfly Bush (Buddleja davidii)

Features Details
Type Perennial
Zone 5-8 (mulch, protect in winter)
When it Blooms June-August
Where to Grow Full sun, partial sun
Soil Type Well draining, moist, drought resistant after first year
When to divide Cannot divide
Habit Height: 24” first year, then 3-6', Spread: 12” first year, then 3-4'. Shrub that dies back during winter
Scent Very sweet
Attracts Butterflies, hummingbird moths, bees
Resists Deer and rabbits
Maturity Rate Flowers first year. Takes 2-3 years to reach max height
Long Lived or Short Lived Long Lived
Something Special or Unique Monarch Magnet! Highly nectar producing. Non-invasive variety. In the North, helps monarch with migration-blooms after eggs are set
