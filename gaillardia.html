Arizona Apricot (Gaillardia grandiflora)

Features Details
Type Perennial
Zone 3-9 (mulch in winter to prevent frost heave)
When it Blooms Summer (July through first frost)
Where to Grow Full Sun
Soil Type Well Drained, low fertility, drought resistant
When to divide/collect seeds Collect seeds for next year store in cool, dark, dry place
Habit Height: 10”, Spread: 12”, Clumping
Scent None
Attracts Butterflies
Resists Deer and rabbits
Maturity Rate Flowers first year, may be leggy first year, but dense clump second year
Long Lived or Short Lived 2-3 years, but a few reseeds each year. Seeds easy to collect
Something Special or Unique Extensive bloom time 2-3 months
