Features Details
Type Tender succulent (not winter hardy)
Zone 10
When it Blooms July-August
Where to Grow Full shade outdoors; eastern or western window, does not like direct sunlight
Soil Type Well draining, low fertility, drought resistant
When to divide When rosette has at least six leaves. Take a clean scissors and cut from main plant. Do not water for 2 weeks after transplanting to prevent roots from rotting
Habit Height: 6-8”, Spread: 6”, Clumping forming rosettes
Scent Small sweet sappy/sticky white flowers
Attracts
Resists
Maturity Rate
Long Lived or Short Lived Long lived
Something Special or Unique Only likes to be watered about once per month, likes to thoroughly dry out between waterings. Grow roots during the winter, mostly dormant during the summer
