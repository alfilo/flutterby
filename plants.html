The links below open detail pages in new tabs. Click checkboxes next to links to graph zones and bloom periods for the selected plants (see graphs below). "Select All" adds all plants in the list below; "Clear Selections" removes all plants. When no plants are selected, graphs use the list below.

In order to reduce the usage of plastic, some plants are planted in cloth pots. These pots are made from 100% undyed, unbleached, organic cotton. The plants can be planted directly in the soil and the roots will grow through the pot while it composts.
