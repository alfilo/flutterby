May Night (Salvia sylvestris)

Features Details
Type Perennial
Zone 3-9
When it Blooms Late May - June (Early to mid-summer)
Where to Grow Full sun, drought resistant
Soil Type Any soil, but must be well draining, does not tolerate soggy winter soil
When to divide Rosettes can be removed in early spring
Habit Height: 18”, Spread: 18”, Clumping
Scent None
Attracts Butterflies and Hummingbirds
Resists Deer and rabbits
Maturity Rate Blooms first year
Long Lived or Short Lived Long lived
Something Special or Unique Difficult to grow from seed. Will flower late first year, and return strong next year. Remove spent flowers to promote reblooming
